电镀商品颜色脱落问题

雨天2023-11-232023-12-14

您好， 感谢您与我们联系。这款石头表面电镀金属颜色（非纯金），电镀商品避免与任何液体接触，例如生活用品的液体。汗水、海水、等，避免在睡眠时，淋浴时佩戴。这些场景都会对电镀层表面造成一定影响。在当时颜色不会立刻脱落，随着时间的推移会发生脱落反应。
我们建议，在销售珠宝时做好产品说明工作。
我们建议为您的用户提供全新的更换服务，
我们将提供您5$折扣代码。稍后发送到您的账户。，

Hello, Thank you for contacting us. This stone surface plating metal colour (not pure gold), plating goods avoid contact with any liquid, such as the liquid of household products. Sweat, seawater, etc., avoid wearing it while sleeping, showering. All these scenes will have some effect on the surface of the plating layer. At the time the colour will not come off immediately, over time a peeling reaction will occur.
We recommend that you do a good job of explaining your products when selling your jewellery.
We recommend that you offer your customers a brand new replacement.
We will offer you 5$ discount code. sent to your account later. Please contact us for more information.
