包裹运输问题

Hello, this package has been shipped in your country for a long time. Please arrive at the local post office in the near future and ask if there is your package. If you have any news, please contact us. Thank you.

你好，这个包裹已经在你们国家运输很长时间了。请在近期前往当地的邮局，询问是否有你的包裹。如果您有任何消息，请与我们联系。谢谢你！

Hello, dear, I have checked your order, the package has been shipped in your country and is expected to be received soon. Please don’t worry, thank you 😘

你好，亲爱的，我查看了你的订单，包裹已经在你的国家运输了，预计很快就会收到。请不要担心，谢谢😘

纠纷介入问题

Hello, I see your order opened. By the way, I checked your order, and the package shows that it has been delivered to the local post office. Please try to contact the local post office for information about your package,

Tracking number: 0000000000000

If you have any postal information, please contact me,

你好，我看到你的订单打开了纠纷。顺便说一下，我查了一下你的订单，包裹上显示它已经送到当地邮局了。请尽量与当地邮局联系，了解您包裹的信息。

运单号:0000000000000

如果您有任何邮政信息，请与我联系。

商品材质问题

Hello, this product itself is made of natural black hematite (non-magnetic), the surface of the product uses vacuum plating color, the color is very bright and beautiful, Thank you

您好，本产品本身采用天然黑色赤铁矿(无磁性)制成，产品表面采用真空镀色，颜色非常鲜艳美观，谢谢
